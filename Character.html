DWARVES

“ Winter is coming ”

They mined precious metals throughout the mountains of Middle-earth. The 7 different groups of Dwarf originated in the locations where the Seven Fathers of the Dwarves awoke before the First Age. The dwarves were smaller and more unsociable Dwarves several houses,which had been banished in ancient time. They were aggressive, and fierce in battle.

ELVES

“ Elf always pay its debts ”

Born under the stars before the ascension of the Moon and the Sun, they retain a special love for light and an inner spirit endowed with unique gifts. Fair and fine featured, brilliant and proud, immortal and strong, tall and agile. They can see as well under moon or starlight as a man at the height of day. They cannot become sick or scarre their spirit goes to the halls of Mandos and as they are bound to Arda and cannot leave until it dies,

THE BAD

" Hear me roar! "

The race first bred by Morgoth, which mostly lived in mountain caves and disliked sunlight. Many of them lived in the Misty Mountains while others lived in Mordor. They are also known as goblins. The Orcs weren’t created, since "evil cannot create, only corrupt" in Tolkien's philosophical perspective.
